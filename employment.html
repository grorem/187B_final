Employment Law

If you are an employee, then you have rights that are protected under State and federal laws. I have defended clients in many employment law cases including:
  • Discrimination
  • Wrongful termination
  • Wage and overtime claims
  • Breach of contract
Case highlight: In a recent case, I have represented an employee who alleged a major corporation had terminated him for reasons of age and religion. Although the corporation denied it, I successfully helped my client reach an agreeable settlement.
