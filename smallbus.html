Small Businesses

I have formed corporations, partnerships and limited liability companies for small businesses. Over the years, these have varied from entities owned by one person who needed the protection of a limited liability company or a corporation to a few people who needed to structure their business relationship. I have advised those businesses in transactions including contracts and leases. In some instances, the client needs a contract drafted, in others the client has been presented with a proposed contract and needs review and representation in finalizing the contract.
