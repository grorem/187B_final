Accidents & Injuries

If you have been involved in an accident or have been injured, you have the right to be compensated for your losses. I have won cases for clients who have needed legal aid in:
  • Automobile accidents
  • Slip and fall
  • Wage and overtime claims
  • Breach of contract
  • Medical device failure litigations
Case highlight: in one memorable case, a retailer had a problem with the automatic doors that opened as a customer approached the business. Unbeknownst to the retailer, the company that serviced the door disabled the mechanism that kept the door open while a person passed through the doorway. As a result, the door only stayed open a few seconds, which would be enough for most people to pass through the doorway. An elderly woman who moved slowly, using a walker, was knocked to the ground and injured when the door closed on her since the electric sensor that should have kept the door from closing had been disabled.
