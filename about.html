Mark O. Rorem has been more than 30 years of experience in the law industry. He has successfully represented individuals in a wide array of cases, with emphasis on cases related to employment law, contracts, small businesses, accidents and injuries, and landlords.

He has great passion in helping others. He has contributed many pro bono work during his years of practice. In one particular case, he represented a homeless man against a social worker who stole his veteran benefits. Despite the time and work required to successfully win the case, he decided to defend the man at no charge.
