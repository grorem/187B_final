Consultation Process

A good attorney has a clear process to help his/her clients win a lawsuit. The below section explains the simple five-step consultation process with Mark O. Rorem.

  • I
    Introduction
    I will listen to the potential your desired goal or legal issue. I will provide reasons if I conclude that there is no legal case. If I determine that there is a legal case, I will then provide some facts that are relevant to the case; and if necessary, I will find further facts that must be obtained to fully understand the situation.
  • II
    Evaluation
    Once I fully understand your issue or desired goal, I will find relevent aspects of the law to apply to the case and to meet your purpose.
  • III
    Discussion
    I will disccuss with you about the possible actions that you can take, the process that must be followed to meet your goal, and the estimated time and expense to complete the case.
  • IV
    Agreement
    You and I will then agree on the course of action to take.
  • V
    Follow Up
    I will have regular consultations with you to maintain a constant communication to improve the success of your case.
